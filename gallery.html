Video Game / Level Design

The level I designed called "Mictlan's Sacred Spire" during my time with my game design class.

This level actually won me an award at our college art show as I had submitted the top rated game level for that semester.

a lot of moving parts in game design can make it an extremely tedious process, whether or not you have a gamekit to work from, though it helps infinitely. That said I loved the process of designing my level to no end, and even with a relatively simple base game control scheme, I feel I was able to make an immersive, interesting, unique, and elaborate gaming experience that I am, truly, beyond proud of.
