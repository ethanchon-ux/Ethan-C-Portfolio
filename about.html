About Me

ETHAN CHON

Game/Web Designer & Animator

Throughout my life, I’ve only ever had one goal with what I created: Make people happy. I want to create amazement, I want to create satisfaction, I want to create fun, and I want to inspire joy. While the medium matters just as much as the intent, I try to take on a wide spread of skills to feed back into my biggest passion projects, because the more I understand about every kind of art, the more I open my mind to different possibilities in the mediums I like most. I focus on digital art because I’ve always had a fascination with animation, and I’ve grown a great interest in design as a whole. From what I’ve made, and into the future, I am going to continue to pursue exciting, insightful, and meaningful positivity in my drawings, animations, and games.

Biography or About Info

I am a 22 year old designer aspiring to work on a broad variety of projects and within several mediums to expand my horizon and gain as many different design skills as possible. My ultimate goal is to be a fully fledged and published video game designer, as well as be an excellent 2D animator (and beyond).

My focus with my work is to make things as intuitive and interesting as possible. I want to excite and amaze, while also creating softer and subtly crafted works. Most of all I want to evoke the right feelings for the right moments, and make BIG impacts land as firmly and strongly as they can. I want someone to remember seeing what I've created and carry that with them, even if just in the back of their mind.
